About Me

Gaming

I play games, since I was a kid. My first PC gaming experience was with KOTOR-II. I love well written plots, and it has many good side stories. My Cousin showed me how I can cheat. It was funny and I was fascinated about how a some lines of text changes the game.

Before high school I already played World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King. The fantasy setting won my heart. There are many boring and repetitive tasks in wow, so I thought it would be cool to automate. But how the heck do I that? 2 weeks, many apples later I've learned how to make addons to the game. So I wrote my own rotation guide for holy paladin and mining node explorer. It was hard coded, and to be honest buggy, but I've made it and I had fun to learn LUA and playing WoW.

In high school I've got lot of friends of the same interests. We played Age of Empires, Civ5, Counter Strike 1.6, League of Legends, Hearthstone, Call od Duty just to name a few. We had a lot of fun and I have friends from this time till today. In school I've learned CNC machining and machining in general. I was curious about how the digital control of these machines work, so I've learned C and with the help of teachers I could understand, how the system of a CNC machine works.

Last year of high school (2016/17) with one of my friend, we did a patent in multi-nozzle 3D printing. (P1600235 -> http://epub.hpo.hu/e-kutatas/) I learned a lot about microcontrollers, mechatronics and programming in C and Python. And then, I decided to become a Mechatronics Engineer.

Currently, I do gaming as a socializing activity. So if you would like to play LoL, Hunt:Showdown or just chat, hit me up on discord!

Dnd

At Uni, I've met lot of new friends. We mainly played boardgames. We terraformed Mars, conquered Westeros, built up the Seven Wonders, played with Unstable Unicorns and Booming Cats. Many many 6-7 hours game nights, and they were awesome! Every one of them.

At some point we thought that, let's try out Dungeons and Dragons. And Boy, I glad we did it. If you want to live out your creativity in a fantasy setting, just play Dnd. Huge world, Endless lore, great plots, a journey with friends, and dragons.

Since then I was GM, player and I have many characters in the world of Dungeons and Dragons. Currently, I play only as a player. I love mend the wounded, and help the weak, so I play the Cleric Class.
